💡 Troubleshooting Tips

🔴 Ball Not Detected?

  • Increase Hue range (try 0-20 for orange/red)
  • Decrease Saturation Minimum to 50
  • Decrease Value Minimum to 50
  • Make sure ball is well-lit

🟡 False Detections (Wrong objects)?

  • Decrease Hue range (make it narrower)
  • Increase Saturation Minimum to 120+
  • Increase Value Minimum to 100+

🔄 Robot Turns Too Slow?

  • Increase Kp to 1.0-1.5
  • Decrease Ki to 0.01

🎢 Robot Oscillates (Wiggles)?

  • Increase Kd to 0.15-0.25
  • Decrease Kp to 0.5-0.7

  • Saturation: 100-255 (vibrant colors)
